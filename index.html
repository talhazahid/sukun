The Pure Sukún Philosophy

Pure Sukún is the feeling of complete calm. Think of the freshness we feel instantly when we find ourselves in or around water.

That moment in which we are almost mildly meditative and relaxed, a moment which shows the universal power of nature combined with the sensations and sounds that transport us to another world; that’s the feeling of freshness we find ourselves in when we are in or around water. That connectedness, and freshness, is what Pure Sukún is trying to capture with our products.

Our products are built around an ethical philosophy – from sourcing sustainable ingredients to using environmentally-friendly recyclable and biodegradable packaging wherever possible.

We believe businesses have a responsibility to minimize their impact on the planet. Pure Sukún is promoting the less is more way of life.

Key Ingredients

Globally renowned for its soothing and nourishing properties, Aloe Vera is one of the main hydrating ingredients in our skin care staples. It is chock full of minerals, bursting with over 20 skin nourishing stars and (a powerful antioxidant) to help with inflammation.Its excellent affinity with the skin and the way it quickly penetrates into the skin contributes to the development of new skin cells and helps maintain an optimal cell renewal.

Rich in vitamins A and D, beta-carotene, phytochemicals, and antioxidants, Mango Seed Oil is a boon for your skin. It is tremendously beneficial for eliminating dark spots, acne scars and uneven skin tone and treating blemishes.

Because it’s rich in vitamin C, Mango Seed Oil can help encourage a more plump and tighter look. It also promotes a firmer appearance on skin.

Containing about 48% of the daily vitamin C, and other antioxidants, Pomegranates Seed Oil help protect your skin against premature aging such as wrinkles and fine lines. It promotes smooth, firm skin by increasing collagen and elastin production to soften skin. It works for most skin types, and penetrates deeply into the skin to effectively treat dry skin.

An excellent ingredient for skin care that binds tremendous amounts of moisture to skin to improve skin smoothness, elasticity and freshness.

Delivering an outstanding tactile experience and skin revival immediately after application, Hyaluronic Acid strengthens skin to help resist signs of aging, while boosting radiance and smoothing fine lines.

An approved biochemical for immediate skin revival which helps protect from UV and blue light damage and environmental stresses, such as sunlight, pollution, and toxins for beautiful and healthy-looking skin. Moreover, it helps your skin to visibly minimize enlarged pores, improve uneven skin tone, soften fine lines and wrinkles, diminish dullness, and strengthen a weakened surface.

Simulskin is a dispersion of skin-identical lipids made from water, phospholipids, stearic and palmitic acid that serves as a second skin barrier to provide immediate and long-term skin hydration. It helps stimulate natural collagen production to lift, sculpt and smooth skin's appearance

Sourced from sugarcane, NeossanceTM Squalane is clinically proven to combat anti-aging effects to bring more firmness to the skin with visible smoothing effect and reduction in appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. With an exceptional affinity to skin, Squalane absorbs easily and is known to help replenish skin’s barrier keeping it soft, supple and moisturized.
