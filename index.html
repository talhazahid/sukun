The Pure Sukún Philosophy

Sukún comes from Urdu and means tranquility and calm.

Pure Sukún is the feeling of pure and complete calm. Think of the freshness we feel instantly when we find ourselves in or around water. That moment in which we are almost mildly meditative and relaxed, a moment which shows the universal power of nature combined with the sensations and sounds that transport us to another world; that’s the feeling of freshness we find ourselves in when we are in or around water.

That connectedness, and freshness, is what Pure Sukún is trying to capture with our products. Our products are built around an ethical philosophy – from sourcing sustainable ingredients to using environmentally-friendly recyclable and biodegradable packaging wherever possible. We believe businesses have a responsibility to minimize their impact on the planet. Pure Sukún is promoting the less is more way of life.

Founder’s Notes
- CEO, Ayesha Khan

“For me the best kind of self-care comes from the daily ritual of taking care of my skin. This is my time for myself, something I deeply cherish, which is why putting together my own brand was a natural journey for me.”

Skincare brings this almost zen-like feeling which is also what empowers me to be my best, most authentic self. It forms part of the commitment I made to myself over a decade ago – a promise to take care of myself, because taking care of oneself is an on-going life event.

I took the plunge and started my research into putting together a brand. At first, almost all my friends and family’s first question was whether it would be ‘clean’ and ‘organic’. We take clean to mean uber-natural and stripped of everything – even the good science. It took me some time to make them understand that a balance of science and natural ingredients is the only way to go. You learn from the past and look towards the future, this is the ideology and the balance I’ve looked to perfect.

That was my mission and my vision. And that is how Pure Sukún came into being.
