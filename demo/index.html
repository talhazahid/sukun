Philosophy

The Pure Sukún Philosophy

Sukún comes from Urdu and means tranquility and calm. Pure Sukún, hence, means complete and total calm. It is the feeling of freshness and utter tranquility that we instantly achieve when we are at one with ourselves.

In that moment of tranquility and relaxation, the human mind and body becomes one with nature. That's the feeling of freshness we are trying to capture with our products. Our products are built around an ethical philosophy – from sourcing sustainable ingredients to using environmentally-friendly recyclable and biodegradable packaging wherever possible.

Pure Sukún is promoting the less is more way of life.

Founder’s Notes
- CEO, Ayesha Khan

“To me the best kind of self-care comes from the daily ritual of taking care of myself.”

Women wear a ton of hats and balancing those roles and still making time to do what we are passionate about takes a spine of steel, love, hard work and a lot of determination. To me the best kind of self-care comes from the daily ritual of taking care of myself. This is my time for myself; something I deeply cherish. It’s also something which calms me down, brings me joy and helps me focus. This almost Zen-like feeling is also what empowers me to be my best, most authentic self. It forms part of the commitment I made to myself over a decade ago – a promise to take care of myself, because taking care of oneself is an on-going life event. For me, skincare has always been an obsession, which is why putting together my own brand was a natural journey for me.

When I moved to the States and made my home here as an immigrant several decades ago, I met a small community of women who all came from different walks of life. That small community grew tenfold as time passed and in these women – who come from all sorts of different backgrounds – I found my support system. These women who not only served as my very first test subjects with whom I’ve been sharing my skincare secrets but are also those who pushed me to do my best.

I took the plunge and started my research into putting together a brand. At first, almost all my friends and family’s first question was whether it would be “clean” and “organic”. We take clean to mean uber-natural and stripped of everything – even the good science. It took me some time to make them understand that a balance of science and natural ingredients is the only way to go.

You learn from the past and look towards the future, this is the ideology and the balance I've looked to perfect. That was my mission and my vision, and that is how Pure Sukún came into being. Sukún is the answer to almost every problem of the world today.

Key Ingredients

Globally renowned for its soothing and nourishing properties, Aloe Vera is one of the main hydrating ingredients in our skin care staples. It is chock full of minerals, bursting with over 20 skin nourishing stars and (a powerful antioxidant) to help with inflammation.Its excellent affinity with the skin and the way it quickly penetrates into the skin contributes to the development of new skin cells and helps maintain an optimal cell renewal.

Rich in vitamins A and D, beta-carotene, phytochemicals, and antioxidants, Mango Seed Oil is a boon for your skin. It is tremendously beneficial for eliminating dark spots, acne scars and uneven skin tone and treating blemishes.

Because it’s rich in vitamin C, Mango Seed Oil can help encourage a more plump and tighter look. It also promotes a firmer appearance on skin.

Containing about 48% of the daily vitamin C, and other antioxidants, Pomegranates Seed Oil help protect your skin against premature aging such as wrinkles and fine lines. It promotes smooth, firm skin by increasing collagen and elastin production to soften skin. It works for most skin types, and penetrates deeply into the skin to effectively treat dry skin.

An excellent ingredient for skin care that binds tremendous amounts of moisture to skin to improve skin smoothness, elasticity and freshness.

Delivering an outstanding tactile experience and skin revival immediately after application, Hyaluronic Acid strengthens skin to help resist signs of aging, while boosting radiance and smoothing fine lines.

An approved biochemical for immediate skin revival which helps protect from UV and blue light damage and environmental stresses, such as sunlight, pollution, and toxins for beautiful and healthy-looking skin. Moreover, it helps your skin to visibly minimize enlarged pores, improve uneven skin tone, soften fine lines and wrinkles, diminish dullness, and strengthen a weakened surface.

Simulskin is a dispersion of skin-identical lipids made from water, phospholipids, stearic and palmitic acid that serves as a second skin barrier to provide immediate and long-term skin hydration. It helps stimulate natural collagen production to lift, sculpt and smooth skin's appearance.

Sourced from sugarcane, NeossanceTM Squalane is clinically proven to combat anti-aging effects to bring more firmness to the skin with visible smoothing effect and reduction in appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. With an exceptional affinity to skin, Squalane absorbs easily and is known to help replenish skin’s barrier keeping it soft, supple and moisturized.
